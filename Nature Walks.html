Nature Walks 

Botanical & Forest Exploration

• Key things to experience:

  • Walk through the stunning orchid house and spice garden in Peradeniya Botanical Garden
  • Explore themed gardens (Palm Avenue, Great Lawn, Cactus House).
  • Guided rainforest walks with a chance to see endemic flora and fauna.
  •  Birdwatching and small waterfall visits in Makandawa Reserved Forest (Kitulgala)

• Best time to visit: January to March (dry season in this wet zone).


Wildlife & Eco-Tours

• Key things to experience:

  • Trekking through dense rainforest rich in endemic birds and insects.
  • Listen to the unique forest sounds.
  •  Watch crocodiles basking in the sun from a safe distance.
  • Capture sunrise photos from the Ella side

• Best time to visit: January to April, August to September.


Birdwatching & Wetland Experiences

• Key things to experience:

  • Birdwatching boat safari (over 150 bird species).
    (Kalametiya Lagoon Bird Sanctuary & Wetland Park,Anawilundawa Bird Sanctuary,Udawatta Kele Sanctuary)
  • Go early morning for best bird activity.
  • Quiet movement helps in better bird spotting.

• Best time to visit: November to March (migratory bird season).


Family & Wildlife Parks

• Key things to experience:

  • Observe native and exotic animal species in naturalized enclosures.
  •  Combine with a visit to Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage.
  • The Hurulu Eco Park assures you a feast of sightings of leopards, rusty spotted cats, star tortoise, jungles fowl, parrots, and hawks.

• Best time to visit: Morning visits year-round (avoid midday heat).


Scenic Walks & Highland Adventures

• Key things to experience:

  • Hike to World’s End cliff and Baker’s Falls.
  • Spot Sambar deer and endemic lizards.
  • Watch the iconic train pass over the bridge and Walk through tea estates and jungle trails while visitin

• Best time to visit: February to April or July to September.


Tips and Advice

• Before you Travel:

  • Go with a guide for safety and to spot rare species.
  •  Prepare for leeches—wear leech socks or apply saltwater.
  •  Sunset is the best time for sightings.
  •  Quiet movement helps in better bird spotting.
  •  Combine with nearby attractions for a full-day trip.
  • Arrive before 6:30 AM for best visibility. 
  •  Bring warm clothing—it’s chilly early morning.
  •  Check train schedules in advance for perfect photo moments.(nine arch bridge)
