Diverse Destinations

Your ‘Must-See’ Places

With myriad places to visit, Sri Lanka is a little paradise isle full of surprising variety. You can swap the chaos of a city for a tranquil beach in an hour or less, or switch from tropical heat to cool heights and cloud forests for a few more. There are mountains to climb, waves to surf, whales to watch, history to revisit, jungles to explore, even elephants to meet. The destinations in Sri Lanka are wide and varied and we’ll gladly share them with you in the very best way.

Kelaniya Rajamaha Viharaya

A sacred Buddhist temple steeped in history and legend, famed for its vibrant murals and serene atmosphere. A must-visit spiritual haven just outside Colombo, offering a peaceful retreat and a glimpse into Sri Lanka’s rich cultural heritage.

Colombo National Museum

Step into Sri Lanka’s past at the island’s largest museum, showcasing priceless artifacts, royal regalia, and ancient art. A captivating journey through history, perfect for culture lovers and curious travelers alike.


Guest Houses Travel
Dutch Hospital Shopping Precinct

Experience historic charm meets modern vibes in this beautifully restored colonial complex. Enjoy boutique shopping, trendy cafes, and vibrant nightlife—all set within walls that whisper tales of Sri Lanka’s Dutch heritage.

Pahiyangala Caves 

Journey back 37,000 years at one of South Asia’s oldest prehistoric sites. These towering limestone caves, linked to a Chinese monk's legend, offer breathtaking views and a deep dive into Sri Lanka’s ancient human history

Guest Houses Travel
Thudugala Ella Waterfall

A hidden gem nestled in lush jungle, perfect for a refreshing dip and a nature escape. With its natural rock pool and charming forest trail, this waterfall offers serenity just off the beaten path.


Guest Houses Travel
Sri Dalada Maligawa

Home to the sacred Tooth Relic of the Buddha, this stunning temple in Kandy is a spiritual heart of Sri Lanka. With golden roofs, serene lakeside views, and centuries of devotion, it’s a must-visit cultural treasure.

Guest Houses Travel

Sigiriya

Climb the iconic Lion Rock and uncover an ancient royal fortress in the sky. With stunning frescoes, water gardens, and 360° views, Sigiriya is a masterpiece of history, art, and adventure.


Guest Houses Travel
Royal Botanic Gardens, Peradeniya

Wander through 147 acres of blooming beauty, exotic palms, and spice-scented trails. Just outside Kandy, this historic garden is a floral paradise perfect for peaceful strolls and nature photography.

Guest Houses Travel
Horton Plains National Park

Trek through misty highlands to reach the dramatic cliff edge of World’s End. With wild sambar deer, cloud forests, and crystal-clear streams, this park is a nature lover’s dream at the top of Sri Lanka.
