Water Sports

Jet Ski

• With picturesque landscapes, lush palm trees, pristine golden shores, vibrant green waters, and consistently warm temperatures, Sri Lanka ranks among the top islands globally for aquatic adventures. Jet sking stands out as one of the most exhilarating water sports for enthusiasts who enjoy competing with powerful waves.

• Best Locations: Bentota is the centre of all the water sports in Sri Lanka. Located in the Galle district, it provides all sorts of water sports opportunities. Negombo is another location just 15 Km away from Colombo international airport.

• Best time to visit: October to April on the south-west coast and March to November on the north-east coast.

Diving

• Dive into the vibrant underwater world of Sri Lanka, where sunken shipwrecks, coral gardens, and colorful marine life await. Whether you're exploring mysterious wrecks teeming with fish, gliding through warm Indian Ocean currents on a scuba dive, or snorkeling along the southern coast’s crystal-clear reefs. Every experience is unforgettable. Encounter everything from majestic blue whales to tiny nudibranchs, lionfish, and rays.

• Best time for Scuba diving: For west and south-west coast then October to May is the best time. However, May to October is the ideal time for the northeast coast.

• Best time for Snorkelling: The best time to indulge in this activity is March, April or May.

Kayaking

• Paddle through Sri Lanka’s serene waters and discover a different side of the island; one where nature, adventure, and tranquility meet. Whether you’re kayaking along the peaceful Kalu Ganga, canoeing through the lush Unawatuna Lagoon, or exploring the vast beauty of Samanalawewa reservoir, every stroke brings you closer to untouched landscapes, exotic birds, and mangrove forests. Looking for family fun? Try the thrilling banana or tube rides; low-risk, high-adrenaline water sports perfect for kids and grown-ups alike.

• Best time for Lagoon Canoeing: Morning or Evening time is suitable for this tour.

• Best time for kayaking: When it is less windy, October to early May will be a good time to visit.

• Best time for Banana & Tube Ride: The best time to visit is from October to April with less powerful waves and lesser precipitation.

Surfing

• Ride the waves and dive into one of Sri Lanka’s most thrilling adventures — surfing! From beginner-friendly Weligama to world-famous Arugam Bay and Hikkaduwa, the island offers perfect surf spots for every level. Lessons with local instructors ensure a fun and safe experience.

• Best time for Surfing: During November and April.

•Craving an adrenaline rush? Head to Kitulgala, one of Sri Lanka’s wettest and wildest regions, for an unforgettable white water rafting adventure. Just 90 km from Colombo, the Kelani River offers thrilling grade 3 rapids, ideal for both beginners and thrill-seekers. Surrounded by lush rainforest, this experience combines heart-pounding action with stunning natural beauty. Perfect for anyone ready to ride the rapids and feel truly alive.

Tips and Advice

• Before you Travel:

  • Always wear a life jacket, especially when participating in activities like kayaking, jet skiing, or white-water rafting
  • Seek advice from experienced locals or instructors about currents, tides, and potential hazards in the area
  • Be aware of monsoon seasons and their impact on different coasts.
  • If you're new to water sports, begin in calmer areas like lagoons or rivers before venturing into the ocean
