Safari

Yala National Park

• Key things to experience:

  • Spotting Sri Lankan leopards (Yala has one of the highest densities in the world)
  • Watching elephants, sloth bears, and wild boars in their natural habitat
  • Birdwatching – flamingos, eagles, peacocks, and more

• Best time to visit: February to July (Dry season, best for wildlife sightings at waterholes)

 

Udawalawe National Park (South-Central)

• Key things to experience:

  • Close-up encounters with large herds of wild elephants
  • Visiting the Udawalawe Elephant Transit Home (orphaned elephant rehabilitation)
  • Stunning photo ops near Udawalawe Reservoir

• Best time to visit: May to September

 

Wilpattu National Park (Northwest)

• Key things to experience:

  • Exploring "Willus" (natural rainwater lakes unique to Wilpattu)
  • Spotting leopards, barking deer, and rare water birds
  • Less touristy – more natural and raw wildlife feel

• Best time to visit: February to October

 

Minneriya National Park (North-Central)

• Key things to experience:

  • Witnessing “The Gathering” – 200+ elephants come to Minneriya Tank
  • Watching baby elephants, tuskers, and family herds interact
  • Birdwatching and spotting crocodiles near water

• Best time to visit: July to October, especially for The Gathering

 

Kaudulla National Park (Close to Minneriya)

• Key things to experience:

  • Elephants migrating from Minneriya – up-close and personal encounters
  • Safari rides across open grasslands and lakes
  • Crocodiles, sambar deer, and exotic bird species

• Best time to visit: August to December

 

Tips and Advice

• Before you Travel:

  • Bring a hat, sunglasses, and light cotton clothing for daytime heat.
  • Carry extra batteries and memory cards.
  • Don’t forget a water bottle and sunscreen.
  • Keep noise to a minimum to avoid scaring wildlife.
  • Never feed or try to touch the animals.
  • Go with licensed guides and drivers who follow conservation rules.
  •  Stick to designated safari routes.
  • Carry basic medication if you're prone to motion sickness or allergies.
